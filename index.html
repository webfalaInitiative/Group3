ABOUT US

WHY PROTECTIVE ECHO?

Community Support: Protective Echo is more than a reporting platform, it is a community committed to fostering empathy,
understanding and solidarity.

Advocacy for Change: By amplifying voices and stories, we aim to contribute to a cultural shift, braking down barriers and fostering a society free from sexual and gender- based violence.

Anonymous Empowerment: We priortize your safety and confidentiality, allowing you to share your experiences without fear of judgement or retribution.

OUR MISSION

At PROTECTIVE ECHO, we believe in the power of collective action to addrees and eradicate sexual and gender- based violence. Our platform is dedicated to providing a safe and anonymous space for survivors and witnesses to share their stories, report incidents and find support.

OUR VISION

At PROTECTIVE ECHO, WE envision a world free from sexual and gender- based violence, where every individual feels safe, heard and empowered. Our vision extends beyond breaking the silence; in encompasses fostering lasting change in societal attitudes..

Together we stand against silence

We are determined to help you speak out ,take action and support you in times of silence or oppression.
