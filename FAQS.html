Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Protective Echo?

Protective Echo is a platform dedicated to providing a secure and anonymous space for reporting and addressing sexual and gender- based violence.

2. How does the anonymous reporting work?

Our reporting process allows users to share experiences without revealing their personal information.

3. How is my anonymity protected?

We use encryption and other security measures to protect user data. No personally identifiable information is required for anonymous reporting.

4. Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Our platform employs industry- standard security practices to safeguard user information.

5. What services do you offer?

We provide resources such as helpline, counseling, legal support, and a community forum.

6. Can I volunteer or contribute in other ways?

Yes, we welcome volunteers and contributors.
