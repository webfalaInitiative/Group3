Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is Protective Echo?

Ans: Protective Echo is a platform dedicated to providing a secure and anonymous space for reporting and addressing sexual and gender- based violence.

2. How does the anonymous reporting work?

Ans: Our reporting process allows users to share experiences without revealing their personal information.

3. How is my anonymity protected?

Ans: We use encryption and other security measures to protect user data. No personally identifiable information is required for anonymous reporting.

4. Is my data secure?

Ans: Yes, we take data security seriously. Our platform employs industry- standard security practices to safeguard user information.

5. What services do you offer?

Ans: We provide resources such as helpline, counseling, legal support, and a community forum.
