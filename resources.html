RESOURCES

Welcome to the resources section of Protective echo. Here, we aim to provide a collection of tools, information and support networks to empower survivors and allies on their journey towards healing and awareness.

Educational Material On SGBV

The Body Keeps the Score by Bessel Van Der Kolk explores the effect of trauma on the body and mind, offering insights into understanding and healing from experiences like SGBV. It delves into the role of therapy and various therapeutic approaches.

I Will Survive by Lori Bryant- Wool-ridge is a collection of personal stories from survivors of various traumas, providing inspiration and guidance on overcoming challenges. It emphasizes resilience and the journey towards healing.
